Nature refers to the physical world and the natural environment, including all living organisms, landscapes, ecosystems, and non-living elements such as air, water, and minerals. It encompasses the Earth's natural resources, biodiversity, and the complex interactions between organisms and their surroundings.

Nature is characterized by its diversity and complexity. It includes various ecosystems, such as forests, deserts, grasslands, oceans, and wetlands, each with its unique set of species and ecological processes. These ecosystems provide habitats for a wide range of plants, animals, and microorganisms, contributing to the overall balance and sustainability of our planet.
